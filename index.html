Pong

This is the classic game Pong (complete with AI). Don't let the ball pass through your side of the screen by deflecting it with the paddle. First one to 7 points wins!

Breakout

This game is called breakout. Try to destroy the coloured bricks at the top by bouncing the ball off of the movable brick at the bottom. If the ball passes through the bottom of the screen, you lose a life. You have 5 lives before it's game over!

Snake

This game is called Snake. Try to avoid bumping into yourself as the snake grows. You get points over time and you also get 100 points for each piece of food that you eat. Note: You can go through the outer walls and come out on the other side of the screen.

Connect Four

This game is called Connect Four. Two players are required for this game. Take turns placing your colored pieces in the different columns. The first player to connect four pieces vertically, horizontally, or diagonally wins!

Hangman

This game is called Hangman. Try to guess the word by selecting individual letters. You only have a certain number of wrong guesses before the man is hanged!
